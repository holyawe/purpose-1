The Dark Side of Beauty.

Beauty has become a lot like fast fashion, which involves an endless supply of products and puts pressure on consumers to purchase more and leads them to hyper-consumerism in the skincare industry.
Well, marketing tactics can be tempting, but it’s important to know that you don’t need to overload your skin with a 10-step pricy complex routine that can actually lead you to more problems, such as breakouts, redness, irritation and dryness.

Our Purpose:
Redefining Skincare

We knew there had to be a better way, a more mindful approach to skincare that focuses on the essentials, harnesses the safest and most effective ingredients.

That's why Purpose Skin Lab was born. We are here to disrupt the beauty industry, to slow it down, with a safe and minimalist approach to skincare, and to promote the concept of "less is more".
